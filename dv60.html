Dominic Verity's 60th Birthday Conference

Baltimore • July 20 – 22, 2026

About DV60

This conference celebrates the 60th birthday of Dominic Verity, honoring his significant contributions to category theory and homotopy theory. The conference will take place immediately following CT2026, providing an excellent opportunity for the category theory community to celebrate Verity's work and impact on the field.
