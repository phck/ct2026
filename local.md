Suggestions from local organizers for eating, drinking, and exploring around the Homewood campus and Baltimore. All within a short walk or transit ride of campus unless noted.

## Lunch Near Campus

* **[&pizza](https://andpizza.com/locations/baltimore-md-charles-village/)** — fast-food personal pizza (eat in or take away)
* **Ajumma** — Korean
* **[Bird in Hand](https://www.birdinhandcafe.com/)** — sandwiches and salads
* **Carma's Café** — soups, sandwiches, salads, and more, in the Space Telescope Science Institute
* **Charles Village Market** — grocery store for students, with some prepared-food options
* **[Charles Village Pub](https://charlesvillagepubbaltimore.com/)** — pub / dive bar (decent food with those expectations)
* **Chipotle Mexican Grill** — fast-food burritos and bowls (eat in or take away)
* **[Dear Charles](https://www.dearcharles.com/)** — Mid-Atlantic cuisine
* **[Doppio Pasticceria](https://www.doppiobaltimore.com/)** — Italian deli (limited indoor seating, nice picnic tables outside)
* **[Gertrude's](https://www.gertrudesbaltimore.com/)** — sit-down restaurant in the Baltimore Museum of Art
* **[HomeSlyce](https://homeslyce.com/)** — pizza and much more (eat in)
* **Honeygrow** — salad bowls and stir-fries (very quick)
* **[Kajiken](https://www.kajiken-usa.com/)** — Japanese (eat in; very popular for seminar lunches)
* **[Kitsch](https://www.kitsch.cafe/)** — café with sandwiches to take away
* **Kyodai Express** — sushi
* **Levering Kitchens** — food court in the basement of Levering Hall, with various options
* **One World Café** — salads and sandwiches; good vegetarian options
* **[Orient Express](https://www.orderorientexpress.com/)** — Chinese (eat in or take away)
* **[R. House](https://r.housebaltimore.com/)** — food court with many options; can accommodate a large group
* **[Sakoon](https://www.sakoonindianfusionmd.com/)** — Indian (lunch buffet)
* **[Sweet27](http://www.sweet27.com/)** — Indian-fusion bakery and restaurant (gluten-free baked goods)
* **[Tamber's](https://www.tambersrestaurant.com/)** — Indian and more (can accommodate a larger group)
* **[THB](https://www.eatthb.com/)** — bagels and sandwiches
* **[The PaperMoon Diner](https://papermoondiner24.com/)** — sit-down diner

**Further afield — Hampden** (~25 minutes' walk): many options along W 36th Street.

## Coffee & Tea

* **[BLC Café](https://www.blccafe.com/)** (Daily Grind, Brody Learning Commons) — near Krieger Hall; 7:30 am–3:30 pm, M–F; breakfast sandwiches and limited packaged lunch
* **[Café Los Sueños](https://cafe-los-suenos.square.site/)** — fancy coffee
* **Daily Grind (Mudd Hall)** — 8:00 am–2:00 pm, M–F
* **[Equitea](https://equitea.com/)** — matcha (Wednesday–Sunday only)
* **[KANOM Café](https://kanomcafe.net/)** — coffee and pastries
* **Kung Fu Tea** — bubble tea (also fried chicken)
* **[Mocha and Pearl Café](https://www.mochaandpearl.com/)** — in the hotel
* **Starbucks** — the ubiquitous chain, with a surprising variety of cold drinks

## Ice Cream

* **[The Charmery](https://www.thecharmery.com/)** (Hampden) — ice cream, with vegan options
* **[Taharka Brothers](https://taharkabrothers.com/)** — in R. House and in the Inner Harbor, with vegan options
* **Rita's Italian Ice** — near the Rotunda and in the Inner Harbor; Italian ice and soft serve

## Culture

* **[Baltimore Museum of Art](https://artbma.org/)** — Baltimore's premier fine-art museum; free to visit, located on campus
* **[The Charles](https://thecharles.com/)** — movie theater with new releases and revivals
* **[Baltimore Farmers Market](https://farmersmarketbaltimore.org/)** — downtown, Sundays 7 am–12 pm; very lively under the highway, with lots of food

## Bars

Several favorites cluster along **23rd Street**, making for an easy crawl:

* **W.C. Harlan's** — speakeasy; nice and quiet early in the week
* **[Fadensonnen](https://www.fadensonnen.com/)** — German-style beer garden with indoor and outdoor seating
* **[Dutch Courage](https://dutchcouragebar.com/)** — gin bar with lots of food options and indoor/outdoor seating
* **[Mama Koko's](https://www.mamakokos.com/)** — cocktail bar with various bar snacks

Elsewhere:

* **[Peabody Heights Brewery](https://peabodyheightsbrewery.com/)** — local brewery with a fantastic arcade-game setup
* **[Bar Dalí](https://www.bardalibaltimore.com/)** — classic cocktails, wine, and tapas

## Dining

* **[Clavel](https://barclavel.com/)** — Oaxacan-style Mexican; very crowded at peak times, great mezcal list
* **[Mera Kitchen Collective](https://www.mera.kitchen/)** — fusion dinner spot in Mount Vernon
* **[Ekiben](https://ekibenbaltimore.com/)** — counter-serve buns and rice bowls; very casual but delicious

## Nature

* **[Stony Run](https://www.stonyrun.org/)** — park immediately west of campus, with nice trails (but stay out of the water!)
* **[Druid Hill Park](https://druidhillpark.org/)** — swimming pool, tennis courts, greenhouse, disc golf, and lots of deer
* **[Lake Roland](https://www.lakeroland.org/)** — north of campus, accessible on the light rail; lots of shaded trails (but stay out of the water!)
